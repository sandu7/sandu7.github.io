In the territory of the Water Park is prohibited

 

Photo and video filming for commercial purposes on the territory of the Aquapark. The exclusive right to publish photographic materials, broadcast video films shot at the Water Park, has only the Water Park Administration.

Entrance to the Aquapark with animals, birds, reptiles, fish.

Jumping and diving from the sides, holding each other under water, using acrobatic jumps, holding the breath under water.

Brisk walking or running in the Water Park to avoid falls and injuries.

Abrupt entry into the water pools Visitors with diseases of the cardiovascular system and vascular diseases of the brain.

Those who cannot swim in the pool bowl without lifejackets and other life-saving accessories.

Leaving unattended children on the territory and in the pools of the Aquapark, as the Aquapark Administration is not responsible for the children of visitors.

Smoking throughout the Aquapark, except for specially designated places. For smoking in places not designated for this, the visitor must pay a fine of 5,000 tenge.

Discriminate or show disrespect to other visitors due to differences in nationality, religion, age, social status, etc.

Swimming with her hair loose due to the possibility of injury.

Do not allow unaccompanied persons under the age of 18 (eighteen) years to the Aquapark territory.
